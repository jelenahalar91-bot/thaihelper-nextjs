# Master template — Contact-form & general-inbox firms (19 firms)

Use this short version for website contact forms and general inboxes.
Forms often have character limits and don't render numbered lists well,
so this version drops the "to finalise the listing" block and just asks
them to email you back.

Personalise the **[FIRM NAME]** and **[LISTING URL]** placeholders for
each submission.

---
