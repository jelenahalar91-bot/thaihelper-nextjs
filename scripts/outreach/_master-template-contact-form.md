# Master template — Contact-form firms (16 firms without direct email)

Use this short version for website contact forms. Forms often have
character limits and don't render well with a numbered list, so this
version drops the "to finalise the listing" block and just asks them
to email you back.

Personalise the **[FIRM NAME]** and **[LISTING URL]** placeholders for
each submission.

---

**Subject:** Free listing on ThaiHelper.app — your profile is already live

Hello,

I'm Jelena Hermann, founder of ThaiHelper.app — a platform connecting families in Thailand with trusted household staff. I've just launched a free directory of immigration lawyers, visa agents, and MOU agencies, and I've created a listing for **[FIRM NAME]**:

**[LISTING URL]**

The listing is free, no commissions, no contract. Our users frequently need help with work permits and visa matters for their domestic helpers.

If you'd like to update or improve the listing, please reply to **jelena@thaihelper.app** with your preferred firm name, a short description, contact details, and (optionally) a logo. If we don't hear back, we'll keep the listing up based on publicly available information.

Directory: https://thaihelper.app/directory

Thanks,
Jelena Hermann
ThaiHelper.app
