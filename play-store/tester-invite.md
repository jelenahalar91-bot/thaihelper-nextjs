# Closed-Test Tester Invitation — Email + LINE

Google requires **at least 20 testers** to opt-in to the closed test
for at least **14 days** before a new personal-account developer can
publish to production. Send this invite to your 17 verified employers
(see Supabase → employer_accounts) plus 3–5 friends or family so you
have a buffer.

When you have all 20 opt-ins, you start the 14-day clock and Google
unlocks the "publish to production" button at the end.

---

## How testers join

1. They need a **Google account** (Gmail).
2. They click the **opt-in link** you'll get from Play Console after
   you create the closed test track (Play Console → Testing → Closed
   testing → Create track → Testers tab → opt-in link).
3. They install ThaiHelper from Play Store via that link.
4. They use it occasionally during the 14-day window — Google tracks
   "active testing days" per Google account.

You'll get the opt-in URL only after the closed test track exists
on Play Console. Until then, save this email and add the link in
square brackets right before sending.

## After the 14 days

You'll see a notification in Play Console:
> *"Your app has completed the required closed testing period and is
> ready for production."*

Then: Play Console → ThaiHelper → Production → Create release →
upload the AAB (same one used for closed test) → submit for review.

Google's first-time review for personal accounts is typically 3–7
days. After that you're live.
